# ArtiZen – Digital Artisan Marketplace & Data Analytics Platform

ArtiZen is a data-driven marketplace prototype designed to improve **visibility, pricing transparency, and market access for local artisans**. The project combines **market research, data analytics, and UI prototyping** to explore how technology can help artisans reach wider audiences and increase income opportunities.

---

## Problem

Many local artisans struggle with:

- Limited **digital presence**
- Poor **price discovery and transparency**
- Lack of **direct access to buyers**
- Fragmented market insights

These issues often result in artisans selling through intermediaries at lower margins and missing potential online demand.

---

## Solution

ArtiZen proposes a **digital marketplace ecosystem** supported by **data analytics dashboards** that help identify market gaps and improve artisan reach.

The project includes:

- Research-based insights into artisan market challenges
- Business intelligence dashboards to visualize market opportunities
- Prototype UI flows for an artisan-focused marketplace

---

## Key Features

- **Market Research & Gap Analysis**
  - Identified major challenges in artisan visibility and pricing strategies.

- **Business Intelligence Dashboards**
  - Built interactive dashboards using **Power BI** to analyze potential market trends.
  - Simulated projections suggesting **25–30% improvement in potential market reach**.

- **Marketplace Prototype**
  - Designed conceptual flows for:
  - Artisan product galleries
  - Buyer discovery experience
  - Collaboration and promotion features

- **Data-Driven Decision Support**
  - Demonstrates how analytics can guide marketplace design and pricing strategies.

---

## Tech Stack

- **Python** – Data preprocessing and analysis  
- **Power BI** – Business intelligence dashboards  
- **HTML / CSS / JavaScript** – Prototype interface design

## Project Impact

The research and dashboard insights suggest that digital platforms designed specifically for artisans could:

- Improve **product visibility**
- Enable **direct-to-consumer selling**
- Expand **market reach by 25–30%**
- Support **data-driven pricing strategies**
